About the Doctor

Dr. Joselito Joel R.
Castro Jr., MD

Dr. Joselito Joel R. Castro Jr. is a board-certified ophthalmologist and a Diplomate of the Philippine Academy of Ophthalmology (DPAO). He provides comprehensive eye care services to patients across Metro Manila and surrounding provinces.

With a commitment to excellence in patient care, Dr. Castro offers a wide range of ophthalmic services — from routine eye examinations to advanced surgical procedures — at multiple clinical locations in Quezon City.

  • Doctor of Medicine (MD)
  • Diplomate, Philippine Academy of Ophthalmology (DPAO)
  • Practicing at General Miguel M. Malvar Medical Foundation, Quezon City
  • Affiliated with New Era General Hospital, Commonwealth Avenue
  • Accredited by major HMO and government health programs

What We Offer

Comprehensive
Eye Care Services

Comprehensive Eye Exam

Full evaluation of visual acuity, eye pressure, and overall ocular health. Suitable for routine check-ups and early detection of conditions.

Cataract Evaluation & Surgery

Diagnosis and surgical management of cataracts, restoring clear vision through modern phacoemulsification techniques.

Glaucoma Screening & Management

Tonometry, optic nerve assessment, and visual field testing for early glaucoma detection and ongoing treatment planning.

Retinal Disease Care

Evaluation and management of diabetic retinopathy, macular degeneration, and other posterior segment disorders.

Refraction & Prescription

Accurate determination of refractive errors — myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism — with updated lens prescriptions.

Pediatric Ophthalmology

Specialized eye care for children including amblyopia (lazy eye), strabismus, and visual development assessments.
